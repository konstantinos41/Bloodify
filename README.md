# Bloodify
Bloodify is an open source UWP that keeps track of your Blood Donations and helps you share them with Bloodify's network.

### Who can use Bloodify?
Are you a Blood Donor? or Would you like to become one?
Then Bloodify is for you!
Keep track of your Blood Donations, share them with Bloodify's network and watch other people's progress.
Donate frequently and become the most active Donor in the world!
